INSTRUMENTS

Aims and Objectives

Organising theoretical and theme based academic programmes on Indian art and culture for teachers and students.
Conducting Workshops to provide practical training and knowledge in crafts to be incorporated in school curriculum. Various art activities like drama, music, narrative art forms, classical dances, etc. are organized to create an awareness of the regional variations and richness of cultural expressions of our country.

Collecting and developing a library of resources in the form of scripts, digital photographs, audio and video recordings and films with the objective of producing culturally-oriented educational aids to encourage the art and craft forms of rural India and their revival.

Preparing publications and other audio-visual material which attempt to provide an understanding and appreciation of the different aspects of Indian art and culture.

Implementing Cultural Talent Hunt Scholarship Scheme for providing facilities to young talented artists in the 10-20 age group to study one or the other art form.

Origin and Growth

We are aware of the fact that music, dance and fine arts in the ancient times used to command a place of pride and prominence due to classical and technical perfection and excellence and that has associated with the performing and visual arts.

The Indian classical music and dance had been developed by the ancient sages while worshipping nature and these arts in turn covered the entire spectrum of emotions and feelings of human beings.

But with the passage of time, stagnation was noticed in the cultural development due to want of encouragement, interest and patronage. Moreover, cultural activities in the form of dance and drama ware considered as contemptible and harsh among the people resulting in creation of a colossal void.

The need of the hour is in variably to revive and bolster the ancient, traditional art forms which were the most important constituents of Indian culture. GCAC is committed its level best to bear the brunt of continuing in phased manners.

Scope Of Work
  • To acquire, establish, start, aid, run, maintain or manage “Cultural Assessment”, libraries for the benefit of public with the prior approval of the competent authority
  • To arrange and organize “Lectures, Debates, Discussions, Seminars & Excursions” for the diffusion of knowledge and improve the health care of the local people.
  • To study, cultivate and “Demonstrate the Art of Music and Dancing”.
  • To promote and encourage “Advancement of Literary, Cultural & Scientific Education”.
  • To help the “Needy Students” of all communities for the prosecution of studies.
  • To organize “Cultural Programmes” from time to time to remove monotony from daily life
  • To organize “Cultural Workshops” from time-to-time inviting students of different cultural academies to motivate and prepare them for talent hunt.
  • To make a comprehensive assessment of the performances of the students so “Invited by the Organization” from time to time.
  • To “Distribute Scholarships” to the talented and poor students for continuing their classes smoothly.
  • To organize cultural “Talent Hunt” and different cultural aspects at regular intervals and infuse competitions attitude among the students.
  • To organize “Cultural Exhibitions” at regular intervals on different cultural aspects i.e. Sangeet, Music, Dancing, Drama, Recitation etc.
  • To help the “Aged, Sick, Helpless and Indigent Artists”
  • To publish or cause to be published “Useful Literatures, Magazines”, etc without profit motive
  • To arrange “Sambardhana to the Legend and Eminent Artists of India” from the various cultural field.
  • To import and develop various “Awareness programmes” like as social awareness, health awareness, environmental awareness among the people of our locality by organizing awareness camps from time to time.
  • To educate the people about “Pollution and to arrange Plantation of Trees” in the locality to make the world green.
  • To provide a common platform for socio-economic, environmental, educational, “Cultural and Legal Advancement” of people and general community through people efforts.
  • To help to organize self-Help Group based programme for augmentation of the “Livelihood Opportunities” of the people at the grass root level as being beneficial to the public or to a section of public.
  • To arrange and organize various programs to the help the needy people of the society “Without Profit Motive”.

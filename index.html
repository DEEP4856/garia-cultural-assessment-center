GARIA CULTURAL ASSESSMENT CENTRE

We are dedicated to promoting cultural education and assessments. The GARIA CULTURAL ASSESSMENT CENTRE (GCAC) is one of the premier Assessment Boards working in the field of cultural education. Established in 2020, it functions as an autonomous organization under the registration of the Societies Act, 1961. In the philosophical core of the GCAC lies a commitment to emotional and spiritual development of the different cultural centres of India, particularly those who run their cultural centre with different aspects of cultural education. To this end, the GCAC conducts Assessment of cultural education like Vocal Song, Dance, Painting, Recitation, Drama, Instrumental music, etc., with cultural knowledge and understanding as conducive to clarity, creativity, independence of thought, tolerance, and compassion. The GCAC also creates special privileges for physically and mentally challenged candidates.
